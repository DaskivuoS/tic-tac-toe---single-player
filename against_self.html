This game is played by the computer against itself
To start the game, you may simply press the button and the first player starts by taking the most advantageous point, the central cell
Also, you can manually select any cell if you want the game to begin from there and the computer shall go on from there
You may also want the computer to start but not necessarily select the central cell, in that case, there is a button at the end of the table
Player 1 is assigned the sybmol -> x
Player 2 is assigned the sybmol -> o
